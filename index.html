About Binura

His player number is 71.

About Binura's Bowling

Binura is a Sri Lankan cricketer left-arm medium fast bowler Known for his talent to generate tricky bounce from a height of 6 feet and 7 inches, and swing the ball, Binura Fernando has been part of the Sri Lanka team for the past 8 years, claiming 2 wickets in ODI, 12 in T20I, 60 in First-class cricket, 58 in List A and 81 in T20. He was chosen mainly because of the strength of his performances at DS Senanayake College and the Sri Lanka Under-19 team.
